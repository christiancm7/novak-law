Lonnie Mills - Lead Litigation Attorney

Lonnie Mills is a litigation associate and trial lawyer at Novak Law Firm. His practices focuses on civil litigation as it relates personal injury matters. A cum laude graduate of Texas Southern University's Thurgood Marshall School of Law, Lonnie has served on the board of TMSL's Law Review as Lead Articles Editor. He is a former prosecutor in Houston, Harris County, Texas and brings with him 16 years of legal experience. Prior to joining Novak Law Firm, Lonnie attended over 3,000 mortgage foreclosure-related appearances on behalf of Bank of America, N.A. amid the subprime mortgage crisis of 2009. Lonnie is licensed to practice law in the State of Texas. During his free time, he enjoys playing piano, adventure, motivational speaking and gospel music.
